**Source**: [Schneider 2013](studienarbeit/schneider-2013-how-to-not-make-the-ekf-fail.md)

$$
\text{MSE}
= \frac{1}{MKN_{n_x}}
\sum_{m=1}^M
\sum_{j=1}^K
\sum_{k=0}^{N-1}
\left(
	\mathbf{\hat{x}}^j(t_k^+)
	- \mathbf{x}^j(t_k)
	\right)^\text{T}
\left(
	\mathbf{\hat{x}}^j(t_k^+)
	- \mathbf{x}^j(t_k)
	\right)
$$

symbols | description
--- | ---
$k$ | time / step
$j$ | how many EKF runs? in tutorial: EKF was ran 1000 times (non-deterministic system due to noise)
