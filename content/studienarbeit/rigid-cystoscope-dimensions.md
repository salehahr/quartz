The sizes of the sheath of the rigid cystoscope are 17 [French gauge](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_catheter_scale) (5.7 mm diameter), 19 Fr gauge (6.3 mm diameter), and 22 Fr gauge (7.3 mm diameter).
